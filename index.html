Scoring System

  • 4 points for a win
  • 2 points for a draw
  • 0 points for a loss
  • +1 bonus point for scoring 20+ points (4+ tries)
  • +1 bonus point for losing by 7 or fewer points
  • Teams tied on points are ranked by points differential (PD)
